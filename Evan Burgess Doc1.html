Finally, time for cookies.

Did I bake cookies this morning? Yes. Yes I did.

They are Seven-Layer Cookies. Seven layers, seven ingredients. In this order:

  • Butter
  • Grahm-Cracker Crumbs
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Butterscotch Chips
  • Coconut
  • Walnuts

Now, you may be thinking, "Okay, that's nice, but how much of each ingredient do I need?", and I would respond in the following manner:

Ingredient How much you should use How much I use
Butter One stick Butter only comes in sticks. Just use one.
Grahm Cracker Crumbs A cup and a half I don't have a measuring cup. Use one package, blus two additional crackers.
Sweetened Condensed Milk One Can Just one can. It's too much work to open another.
Chocolate Chips Half a package Three quarters of a package, and just eat the rest.
Butterscotch Chips Half a package Same as with the chocolate chips. Try eating them together.
Coconut Half a package Just half of a package. Save the rest for the next batch.
Walnuts Half a package A bit less than half a package. I leave a few spots without nuts.

Bake, cool (for four hours), cut, and serve.
